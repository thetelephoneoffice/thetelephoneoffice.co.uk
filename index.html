How It Works:

Our VoIP service provides users with a unique Party Line experience. Each group is assigned one phone number, serving as the caller ID for outgoing calls. Incoming calls prompt callers to enter a 'Party Line ID' (ranging from 1 to 9) on their phone keypad. The selected option directs the call to the corresponding user within the group.

The cost for the service, including one number, is £9.50 per month, divided equally among the members of the group (up to 9 individuals). Each user contributes to the cost share.

Additional Services:

In addition to our VoIP Party Line Service, we offer a range of complementary services, including voicemail, call recording, and advanced analytics to enhance your overall communication experience.

Users have the flexibility to screen incoming calls, allowing them to identify the caller before answering. With unlimited lines, a single user can make outbound calls, while up to two additional inbound calls are routed to the Party Line Number. Additionally, we offer unlimited UK calling.

For added convenience, users have the option to purchase Direct Inbound Dial numbers at £3 per number per month, providing a dedicated line for individual users within the party.
